About my services

My training is ideal for those looking to build muscle, lose fat, or improve functional strength. Your current level of fitness is not important to me, however I do look for clients who are committed, willing to work hard, and understand that results take time and will not be evident from just a few sessions.

I offer in person, one-on-one or small group (2-3) training sessions. I also provide guidance on nutrition and general healthy life habits.

If you are looking for guidance on a more specific or goal, such as improved performance in baseball, or recovering from an ACL tear, it is probably better to search for someone who specializes in that area.

I do not provide any medical advice or provide information on any drug usage, performance-enhancing or otherwise.

Why choose me?

Have you ever been in a gym and noticed a personal trainer who is unenthusiastically guiding their client through different exercises, and doesn't even look to be in very good shape themselves? I certainly have, and it surprises me how common this is.

At bootcamps the trainers are often better; they participate in the workout themselves and are in great shape as a result. But these classes can be very large, a single trainer might be responsible for 20+ people at once. For those looking for more specific guidance and attention this isn't always a good option.

Because I only train individual clients or small groups, I am able to actively engage with my clients throughout the entire workout. I also believe in leading by example, meaning I participate in workouts with my clients..

To me, fitness is a passion first, and a job second, which I believe differentiates me from many trainers out there.

Pricing and options

Initial consultation (~45 minutes) - $25
This is a one-time meeting to assess your goals and develop an understanding of what you are looking to get out of our training sessions. It is also a chance to ask me any outstanding questions. I use the information from this to develop a training and diet plan that is specific to your personal goals, schedule, etc.

Individual training session (75 minutes) - $35
This is a typical training session that involves a 5-10 minute warmup, approximately 60 minutes of resistance training, and a few minutes of stretching and cooldown at the end. The total session should take about 75 minutes, however it will vary slightly depending on the workout and the busyness of the gym.

Small group training session (90 minutes) - $25/person
This is an option if you would prefer to train with up to two other family members or friends. Additional time is allocated compared to the individual session to account for the potentially slower pace of training in group.

FAQ

Where do you train?
I am based out of Toronto, Ontario, and currently have memberships to GoodLife (all locations) and Fortis Fitness West (east end of Mississauga). If you would prefer to train at another location near downtown Toronto I can accomodate that, however you will need to cover any additional costs.

Why are your rates much lower than other personal trainers?
Personal training is not my primary source of income, it is something I do because I enjoy sharing my knowledge and helping others. For this reason I don't care to charge a lot as I want my training to be affordable to many people, and I would rather work with clients who are extremely committed, rather than those willing to pay a lot. Also, because I don't work for a gym or studio, there is no extra margin on top of my fees.

If you don't need the money, why charge at all?
There are two reasons. Firstly, although it is a hobby, personal training still takes up a considerable amount of time. I put a lot of planning into the routines for my clients before showing up at the gym. Secondly, I believe that charging a fee helps to keep both sides more committed: the client because they have financial investment into it, and myself because I want to make sure that every clients gets their money's worth.

When are you available?
Because I work a full-time job through the week my in-person availability is limited, I prefer to train clients on weekends although early mornings before work and evenings are also an option.
