Adding yoga to resistance training: an unexpected benefit

Back in October of 2019 I began suffering from some minor knee pain, which was unusual because it was only in a single knee, and even more so because I had been following the same routine for several months with no issues. At first I tried to ignore it, figuring it was a temporary problem and that it would work itself out. But as the weeks went on it grew from a small discomfort to an intense "pulling" sensation that had me worried that a muscle might tear if I kept on going. I took a few days off from the gym and did some light stretching of the muscles that I suspected might be contributing factors. When I returned to the gym, as I'd hoped my knee felt back to normal, and I could workout again pain-free. But not for long. Surely enough about a week later the pain was back and again I was afraid to workout with full intensity at the risk of injury.
